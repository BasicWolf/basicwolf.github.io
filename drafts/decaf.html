  • Lucaffé Decaffeinato - Roast: medium - Chocolatty, Nutty; 100% Arabica - 42,71 € / kg - 700g This caffeine free alternative from Lucaffé is roasted of the sweetest and creamiest coffees. The goal is to be able to offer the same espresso enjoyment as the caffeinated options. A soft and full-bodied espresso. The coffee is decaffeinated using the methylene chloride method. During the decaffeination process the coffee beans come into direct contact with the solvent, which is then removed through vaporisation. Me: Very strong Arabica taste. Great replacement for normal coffee.
